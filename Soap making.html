About Soap Making

Soap making is the craft of producing soap through the chemical process of saponification, where fats or oils react with lye. It can be done using cold process, hot process, or melt-and-pour methods. Soap makers can customize their products with natural ingredients, essential oils, and artistic designs. This craft is both practical and creative, offering a way to make eco-friendly, skin-safe, and beautifully crafted hygiene products.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Mix lye with water (carefully).
  2. Combine with oils and blend.
  3. Pour into molds and cure for weeks.
