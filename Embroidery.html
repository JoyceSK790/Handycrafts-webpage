About Embroidery

Embroidery is the decorative art of stitching designs onto fabric using a needle and thread or yarn. It has been used for centuries to embellish clothing, linens, and tapestries, often reflecting cultural heritage and storytelling. Techniques vary from simple running stitches to elaborate patterns like satin stitch, French knots, and cross-stitch. Embroidery is both a relaxing hobby and a form of textile art that allows for intricate personalization.
