Candle Making

Crafting candles by melting wax and adding scents or colors.

About Candle Making

Candle making is the process of creating candles by melting wax and combining it with wicks, fragrances, and dyes. Once a necessity for lighting, candles are now used for ambiance, aromatherapy, and decoration. The craft allows for endless customization in shape, scent, and color. Whether using soy, beeswax, or paraffin, candle making is a sensory-rich hobby that blends science with creativity.
