ABOUT US

We propose the establishment of the Handcraft Haven Club, a student-led organization aimed at fostering creativity, practical skills, and community among students. The club will provide a platform for members to explore various handcraft skills, promoting self-expression, innovation, and teamwork.

MISSION & VISION



MISSION

To inspire creativity and empower students through skill-based learning, promoting innovation and sustainability in our community.









VISION

To build a generation of skilled, self-reliant, and innovative individuals who can turn their crafts into careers and create positive change.

Our Crafts


KNITTING

Involves using wool to make different accessories or clothes.

BEAD AND JEWELRY MAKING

Designing and crafting jewelry or decorative items using beads, threads, and other materials. Designing and crafting jewelry using various materials, such as beads, metals, or gemstones.

Woodwork

Crafting objects from wood, such as furniture, decorations, or utensils.

HAIR DRESSING/HAIR CUTS

Learning hairstyles and haircuts using various techniques and materials

FACE MAKEUP

Face makeup as a handcraft work involves using various techniques and materials to create unique, handmade face makeup designs or products

EMBROIDERY

Decorating fabric using needle and thread to create intricate designs or patterns.

SEWING

Creating clothing, accessories, or textiles using a sewing machine and fabric.

PAPER CRAFTING

Creating objects or decorations using paper, such as cards, gift boxes, or origami.

CANDLE MAKING

Creating candles using wax, fragrances, and colorants.

SOAP MAKING

Creating soap using natural ingredients, fragrances, and colorants.

POTTERY

Shaping and firing clay to create ceramics, such as bowls, vases, or sculptures.

UPCYCLING/RECYCLING

Transforming discarded materials into new, useful, or decorative items.
