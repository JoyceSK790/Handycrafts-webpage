Pottery

Shaping clay into vessels or art, then firing to harden.

About Pottery

Pottery is the ancient art of shaping clay into vessels or sculptures, then hardening them through firing. It includes techniques like wheel throwing, hand-building, and slip casting. Pottery is both utilitarian and artistic, producing everything from mugs and bowls to decorative tiles and sculptures. It connects makers to earth and fire, and offers a deeply tactile and meditative experience.

  2. Dry and fire in kiln.
  3. Glaze and refire for finish.
