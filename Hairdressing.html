Hairdressing/Haircuts

About Hairdressing/Haircuts

Hairdressing is the professional and creative practice of cutting, styling, coloring, and treating hair. It blends technical skill with artistic flair and plays a significant role in personal grooming and self-expression. Hairdressers often serve as trendsetters and confidants, working in salons, fashion, film, or freelance. The field requires knowledge of hair types, products, tools, and hygiene, and offers a dynamic career path with opportunities for specialization.
